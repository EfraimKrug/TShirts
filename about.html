About Us (Me, actually. Sorry, there is only one of me.)

I was born in Kansas City.
People ask me why, and I say “I wanted to be close to my mother”.

But seriously folks...

There is such a feeling in the world since this whole COVID thing started. And it is evident everywhere.

Maybe it is fear. Or anger. Something.

I have seen news people say the most important thing is to find somebody to blame. Many people are agreeing with me, when I say something about the common animosity. There's a lot of it about.

It's our goal to fight that animosity. To help people - even in these difficult times - to be kind, considerate, and compassionate. To care for others, even strangers.

The world has always needed more of this, but usually people don't say it because they don't want to be a dweeb.

And now we can just say it.

I’m currently living with two of the most fantastic human beings in the world – they are teaching me more about marriage… what it could be… My constant acknowledgment and gratitude for their friendship and – sheer beauty as human beings. I can’t say enough.

And now, this: Show up in public!
